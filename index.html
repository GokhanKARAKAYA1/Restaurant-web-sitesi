What Is The Secret Of Our Burgers Recipe?

A black and blue burger is a type of hamburger that has blackened or charred seasoning on the outside (hence the "black" part of the name).

To make a black and blue burger, the meat patty is coated with a spicy dry rub that typically includes paprika, cayenne pepper, garlic powder,etc.

Black and blue burgers are a popular variation of the classic hamburger in some parts of the United States.
